Troubleshooting & FAQs

Model Not Downloaded

If the API exists but calls are failing, the local model might not be downloaded. Go to chrome://components, find Optimization Guide On Device Model, and click "Check for update".

Hardware Limitations

If your availability() call returns "unavailable" or throws an error, your device might not meet the hardware requirements (e.g. at least 4GB of RAM and enough free disk space). Setting the bypass perf flag helps in some cases.

Note: The availability() method returns one of these values: "available", "downloadable", "downloading", or "unavailable".

QuotaExceededError

Local models have strict token limits. If you receive a QuotaExceededError, you must chunk your inputs into smaller pieces before sending them to the API. Use the DevTools History panel to view exact token counts for your failing requests.
